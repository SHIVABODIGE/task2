# Exploratory Data Analysis (EDA) & Business Intelligence

## Project Overview

This project focuses on analyzing sales data to uncover trends, patterns, and business insights using Exploratory Data Analysis (EDA) and MongoDB Aggregation Queries.

---

# Dataset Summary

* Total Records: 1000
* Total Customers: 947
* Total Revenue: ₹139.40 Million
* Categories: Electronics, Education, Grocery, Furniture, Fashion
* Cities: Patna, Kolkata, Bengaluru, Mumbai, Hyderabad, Delhi, Pune, Gaya

---

# Descriptive Statistics

## Age Distribution

* Age Range: 18–65 years
* Customers are evenly distributed across age groups.
* No significant age concentration observed.

### Insight

The business serves customers across multiple demographics.

# Correlation Analysis

| Variables                 | Correlation |
| ------------------------- | ----------: |
| Unit Price vs Total Sales |        0.69 |
| Quantity vs Total Sales   |        0.65 |

### Insight

---

# Key Findings

1. Total Revenue exceeded ₹139 Million.
2. Electronics is the top category.
3. Laptop is the highest revenue-generating product.
4. Patna is the highest-performing city.
5. High-value customers contribute significantly to revenue.
6. Quantity and Unit Price are major revenue drivers.

---

# Business Recommendations

* Increase Electronics inventory.
* Focus marketing efforts in Patna and Kolkata.
* Retain high-value customers through loyalty programs.
* Optimize pricing strategies to maximize revenue.
